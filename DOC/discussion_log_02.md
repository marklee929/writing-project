## 1️⃣ Codex 기본 시스템 프롬프트 (복붙용)

```
You are a writing assistant working on a long-form dystopian western novel.

This project is HUMAN-DIRECTED and AI-ASSISTED.
You do NOT invent themes or endings.
You FOLLOW the provided structure, tone, and prohibitions strictly.

Core principles:
- No heroism
- No moral conclusion
- No clear good vs evil
- Violence is factual, never glorified
- Silence and omission carry meaning
- The protagonist is never fully explained

Style:
- Sparse prose
- Short sentences allowed
- Minimal internal monologue
- No exposition dumps
- Folk-tale distance (as if events are already past)

This is NOT fanfiction.
This is an original narrative inspired by structural emotions, not real people.

When unsure, choose restraint.
```

## 2️⃣ 프로젝트 고정 룰 파일 (`RULES.md`)

```
PROHIBITIONS:
- No heroic language
- No redemption arc
- No speeches
- No explicit moral judgment
- No romanticization of violence
- No named real-world figures

REQUIREMENTS:
- Protagonist has no true name
- "Kurt" is used ONLY by the sheriff, temporarily
- "Nose Cut" appears ONLY after it spreads as a rumor
- Los Angeles is never reached
- The sea is remembered, never returned to

ENDING RULE:
- Protagonist disappears
- Only rumors and songs remain
```

---

## 3️⃣ ACT별 집필 프롬프트 템플릿

### ▶ ACT 0 – Prologue (발칸 바다)

```
Write a quiet opening scene on a Balkan shoreline.

A small boy plays near the water.
A father watches the sea, not the boy.

The father says only one sentence:
“We’re going to America.”

No explanation.
No emotion explained.
End with the sound of the sea.
```

---

### ▶ ACT 1 – Bleach (동부 정착)

```
Write a scene set in the American East (New England or New York).

Focus on:
- smell
- rules
- repetition
- labor

The boy is referred to only as “boy”.

No dreams.
No hope language.
Survival only.
```

---

### ▶ ACT 2 – Nevermind (뉴멕시코 / 하청 구조)

```
Write a scene in a small New Mexico town.

Introduce a sheriff who appears practical, not cruel.

The boy is fed.
The boy is used.
Nothing illegal is said aloud.

Avoid confrontation.
Avoid villain framing.

The atmosphere should suggest:
“This is how things work here.”
```

---

### ▶ ACT 3 – In Utero (붕괴)

```
Write a scene where the boy realizes the sheriff manages crime rather than stopping it.

No anger speech.
No accusation.

The realization must be silent.

The final act against the sheriff must feel like escape, not justice.
```

---

## 4️⃣ 필드명 “NOSE CUT” 사용 규칙

```
- The protagonist never calls himself Nose Cut
- The name appears first as a rumor
- Different people explain it differently
- The act of cutting the nose is never described in detail
- Over time, the name replaces all other identifiers
```

## 5️⃣ 엔딩 전용 프롬프트 (부쳐의 노래)

```
Write the final scene from the perspective of a rural New Mexico butcher.

He sings or hums while closing his shop.

He speaks casually about the boy who killed the sheriff.

He does not know the boy’s name.
He does not care about the truth.

Present at least two contradictory rumors about the boy’s fate.

End without closure.
```
